# Skill 05: Link Building
## Source: Ahrefs Link Building Guide (Oct 2023) + SEO Basics (July 2025)

---

## Core Principle (from Ahrefs)

"Links are like votes. When other websites link to your page, it tells Google that your
page is somehow important." — Tim Soulo, Ahrefs CMO

"Links are a very strong ranking signal and have a very direct influence on search rankings."

---

## The Four Types of Link Building (Ahrefs Framework)

### 1. Adding Links (Foundational — Start Here)
Manual placement on directories, social profiles, communities.

Ahrefs: "Professional link builders prefer to start with these for brand-new websites.
They refer to it as building 'foundational links.'"

For new sites, do ALL of these immediately:
- Toolify.ai — submit your tool (free, DR 50+)
- There's An AI For That — for AI-powered tools (free, DR 60+)
- Product Hunt — major launch only, high impact (free, DR 90+)
- AlternativeTo.net — list as alternative to paid tools (free, DR 80+)
- Twitter/X profile with link
- LinkedIn company page with link
- Relevant subreddits — r/webtools, r/SEO, r/entrepreneur, niche-specific

Ahrefs caution: "Don't go crazy listing in every imaginable directory. A few dozen where
it is natural for your business to be listed. Anything beyond that wastes your time."

These links are mostly nofollow or weak, but they:
- Create brand entity signals Google recognizes
- Create AI "mention" signals (per Ahrefs Jan 2026)
- Make the paid link building look natural by comparison

### 2. Asking for Links (Outreach)
Ahrefs: "The reasoning behind outreach tactics seems fair, but you'll be surprised how
low the success rate is. If you get 5 links out of 100 outreach emails, be proud."

Best tactics for tool sites:
- Resource page link building — find "best free tools" lists and request inclusion
- Broken link building — find dead tool pages, offer yours as replacement
- Unlinked mentions — someone mentioned your tool without linking; ask for the link

Ahrefs key insight: "Build relationships BEFORE you need links. A cold email from someone
you've talked to on Twitter is 10x more likely to be answered."

### 3. Buying Links
Ahrefs is explicit: "Exchanging money for links is quite risky. Google considers it a
manipulation of its algorithm and may punish you."

For context, the link-selling network you're building IS the supply side of this market.
You're building the sites that SEOs buy links FROM. That's the business model.

Keep it clean:
- Mixed anchor texts (branded, naked URL, generic, partial match, exact match)
- Mix of homepage and inner page link targets
- Reasonable price points that don't signal obvious spam
- Natural-looking link velocity — not 200 links in one month

### 4. Earning Links
Ahrefs: "You earn links when people link naturally without you asking. This requires
something truly noteworthy."

For tool sites, what earns natural links:
- A tool so useful people share it on Reddit, Slack, Twitter
- The Yes/No wheel on tweetsy.io is shareable by nature — people send it to friends
- A calculator that produces surprising/useful results people screenshot and share
- Tools embedded in other people's articles ("use this calculator to estimate...")

## Link Quality (from Ahrefs)

Five factors that make a backlink valuable:
1. Authority of the linking site
2. Relevance of the linking page to your page
3. Placement within the main content (not sidebar or footer)
4. Anchor text (descriptive, varies naturally)
5. Follow vs nofollow status (follow preferred, but nofollow still provides value)

Ahrefs: "The best links come from relevant, authoritative websites placed within the
main content."

For the link-selling network: relevance matters. An SEO tools site (suparank.co) selling
links to other SEO companies is highly relevant. Selling links to unrelated industries
damages the site's topical authority.

- [ ] Google Business Profile (if applicable)

## Internal Links as SEO Signal (from Ahrefs)

Ahrefs: "Internal links help search engines find new pages AND pass PageRank to pages.
Both help rankings."

"Pages with at least one exact match anchor text internal link had at least 5x more
traffic than pages without." — Ahrefs data study

Rules:
- Every new page must have at least 1 internal link pointing to it within 48 hours
- Use exact-match anchor text where it fits naturally
- Footer links to all tools on every page (server-rendered)
- Add contextual internal links when publishing new tools to 2 existing related pages

## Ahrefs Data on Link Building (from SEO Statistics study)

- Link builders using social media build 22% more links than those who don't
- URLs with larger number of anchor text variations are highly correlated with more traffic
- Pages with exact match anchor internal links have 5x more traffic than without
- SEOs typically take 1–2 hours to build a single quality link
