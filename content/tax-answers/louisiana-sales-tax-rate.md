## ANSWER SECTION

The **Louisiana state sales tax rate is 4.45%**. Combined with **local parish and city taxes**, total sales tax rates range from **4.45% to 12.7%** depending on location. Major cities like **New Orleans** have a combined rate of **9.2%**, **Baton Rouge** has **9.45%**, and some smaller cities like **Ruston** have rates up to **12.7%**.

---

## H2: Louisiana State Sales Tax Rate

**State Rate (2025):**
- **4.45%** on all taxable sales
- Reduced from 5% in July 2018
- Collected by Louisiana Department of Revenue

**State Rate History:**
| Year | Rate | Change |
|------|------|--------|
| Before July 2018 | 5.0% | Base rate |
| July 2018 - Present | 4.45% | -0.55% reduction |

## H2: How Louisiana Sales Tax Works

**Tax Structure:**
```
Total Sales Tax = State Rate (4.45%) + Parish Rate + City Rate
```

**Collection Point:**
- Retailer collects at point of sale
- Remitted to Louisiana Department of Revenue
- Distributed to state, parish, and city

**Special Rate Categories:**

| Category | Tax Rate | Notes |
|----------|----------|-------|
| General sales | 4.45% - 12.7% | Varies by location |
| Vehicle sales | 4.0% + local | Reduced state rate |
| Manufacturing | Exempt | Machinery and raw materials |
| Groceries | Exempt | Unprepared food only |
| Prescriptions | Exempt | All prescription drugs |

- **Tax rate lookup tool:** Enter address for exact rate
- **Mobile app:** Louisiana Sales Tax Calculator

**Factors Affecting Your Rate:**
1. **Parish location** — Each parish sets its own rate
2. **City boundaries** — Some cities add additional tax
3. **Special districts** — Economic development districts may add tax
4. **Type of purchase** — Some items have different rates

**Business Considerations:**
- Collect based on delivery destination address
- File returns with Louisiana Department of Revenue
- May need to file with parish directly in some cases
