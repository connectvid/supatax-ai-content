## H1

Deadline to File Taxes 2025: What Happens If You Miss It

---

## ANSWER SECTION

The deadline to file your 2025 federal income tax return is **April 15, 2026**. If you miss this deadline and owe taxes, you face penalties starting at 5% of unpaid taxes per month. File Form 4868 by April 15 to get an automatic 6-month extension to October 15, 2026.

---

## H2: What Is the Tax Filing Deadline?

For the 2025 tax year (income earned January 1, 2025 through December 31, 2025), the federal filing deadline is **April 15, 2026**.

This deadline applies to:
- Form 1040 (Individual Income Tax Return)
- Form 1040-SR (U.S. Tax Return for Seniors)
- Form 1040-NR (U.S. Nonresident Alien Income Tax Return)

If April 15 falls on a weekend or federal holiday, the deadline moves to the next business day. In 2026, April 15 is a Wednesday, so the deadline is April 15.

---

## H2: What Happens If You Miss the Deadline?

Missing the tax deadline triggers penalties based on whether you owe taxes or are due a refund:

**If you OWE taxes:**
- Failure-to-file penalty: 5% of unpaid taxes per month (max 25%)
- Failure-to-pay penalty: 0.5% of unpaid taxes per month (max 25%)
- Interest compounds daily on unpaid taxes + penalties

**If you're due a REFUND:**
- No penalty for filing late
- You lose your refund if you don't file within 3 years

**Example:** If you owe $5,000 and file 3 months late without an extension:
- Failure-to-file penalty: $5,000 × 5% × 3 = $750
- Failure-to-pay penalty: $5,000 × 0.5% × 3 = $75
- Interest: Additional daily compounding charges

---

## H2: How to Get a Filing Extension

Submit **Form 4868** (Application for Automatic Extension of Time to File) by April 15, 2026 to get an automatic 6-month extension.

**Extension deadline:** October 15, 2026

**Important:** This extends your time to FILE, not your time to PAY. Estimated taxes owed are still due April 15. Pay as much as you can by April 15 to minimize penalties.

You can file Form 4868:
- Electronically through IRS Free File or tax software
- By mailing a paper form
- By making an electronic payment and indicating it's for an extension

---

## H2: State Tax Filing Deadlines

Most states follow the federal April 15 deadline, but exceptions exist:

**California:** April 15, 2026 (matches federal). Form 4868 federal extension automatically extends California filing deadline to October 15.

**Hawaii:** April 20, 2026 (follows federal Emancipation Day observance if applicable)

**Maine and Massachusetts:** April 15, 2026, but state offices may observe Patriots' Day (April 20) affecting processing

**Delaware and Iowa:** April 30, 2026 (extended deadline)

**Virginia:** May 1, 2026

Always check your state's tax authority for the exact deadline.

---

## H2: Special Circumstances and Exceptions

**Military service members:** Those serving in combat zones get automatic 180-day extensions from the last day of combat zone service.

**Natural disasters:** The IRS frequently grants filing extensions to taxpayers in federally declared disaster areas. Check IRS disaster relief notices if your area was affected.

**Living abroad:** U.S. citizens living abroad get an automatic 2-month extension to June 15, 2026. File Form 4868 by June 15 for an additional 4-month extension to October 15.
